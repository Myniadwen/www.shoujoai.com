I Got U

a Card Captor Sakura fanfiction by Masoko-chan

It was a crisp and windy day. Our gang were trying to finish their 
class work. They needed to get it done before the big festival 
tonight. "Sakura, is your costume finished yet?" asked a worried 
Tomoyo. 

"Yes, and its the best one I done so far." replied a cheerful Sakura. 
"I would love to see you in it." Tomoyo stated thoughfully. 

"You'll see it tonight." Sakura commented. "By the way, how's your 
costume coming along?" She asked trying stop Tomoyo's questions.

"It's perfect for tonight." Tomoyo answered then asked, "Have you seen 
Mei-lin and Li?" "No, haven't seen the lovely couple since lunch." 
"You don't think they ran off and got married?" asked Sakura jokingly. 

"No, Li's not the type to do that even with Mei-lin." Then Tomoyo 
said, "Then again, Mei-lin might have snatched him away last night." 
"I wouldn't put it past her." Sakura agreed with a half smile.

"We better stop this giggling or we're never getting through with our 
work." Tomoy suggested trying to reconcentrate on her work. "Sure after 
I get that crazy thought of Li and Mei-lin out of my head." Sakura got 
out finally between gigles. 

Unknown to them however, Mei-lin was right behind Sakura when she said 
that. "And what may I ask are you talking about Sakura? "I'll have you 
know me and Li are destined to be together." Mei-lin practically 
screamed at first, but remembered where she was. 

"Sorry Mei-lin, its just that you were gone all day and ..." "It was a 
bad joke in bad taste." Tomoyo said finishing Sakura's apology. "I'm 
in a good mood so I do forgive you for now." growled the chinese girl 
with fury in her eyes. 

"Truth is, Li just asked me to the festival." Mei-lin said with a 
twinkle in her eye. "I hope you two find dates in time, you know you 
still have 18 hours." The chinese woman smirked with that last 
sarcastic remark before leaving.

"Huh, shows what she knows, you are my date Sakura." "NOOO... You're 
my date remember, I asked you this time sweetie." "So you did my 
angel"

And with no hesitation at all she leaned in for a long and passionate 
kiss.
