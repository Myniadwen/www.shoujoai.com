ARE THEY HAPPY? ARE THEY ANGRY? WILL THEY APPROVE OF THEIR LOVE? 

As they approached Tomoyo's house, Sakura started to get nervous and had 
second thoughts about telling Tomoyo's mom. 

"What if she doesn't like the idea of us being in love and she forbids 
you from seeing me again?"  

"Don't worry Sakura, she'll understand. Even if she does forbid me from 
seeing you, I'll find some way to be with you." 

"Thank You." Said Sakura giving Tomoyo a big hug. 

"I'm home!!" Yelled Tomoyo as she walked in the door. 

"Hi sweetie." Said Tomoyo's mom while giving her a hug. 

"Mom? Sakura and I need to talk to you about something." 

"Ok. But why don't you go upstairs and change first. I'll make some tea 
and wait for you in the living room. Ok?" 

"Alright, come on Sakura." 

Sakura always liked coming over to Tomoyo's house. It was so big and 
beautiful, it made her feel like she was a princess in a fairytale.  

"Sakura? Can you throw my clothes in the hamper for me?" Asked Tomoyo as 
they entered her room. 

"Sure."  

Tomoyo walked over to her closet while Sakura looked through her bag for 
Tomoyo's clothes. She plopped them in the hamper like she was asked. 

"Thanks." Said Tomoyo while taking off the pair of jeans and the 
halter-top that she borrowed from Sakura. 

Sakura picked them up and started folding them while Tomoyo took off her 
bra and Panties that she also borrowed from Sakura. 

"That feels better!" She says, as she slips on her own clothes. 

"Ok, lets go downstairs and have some tea." 

Just like she said, Tomoyo's mom was sitting in the living room, waiting 
with the tea. 

"So? What was it that you two wanted to talk to me about?" 

Sakura tensed up, her fists clenched and her face turning red. 

"Well, said Tomoyo. When I was at Sakura's house yesterday, I asked her 
if she had anything on her mind that she needed to tell me. She had been 
acting strange for a few days and I was worried. When I asked her, I 
knew in my heart what she was going to say." 

She took Sakura's hand in hers for reassurance.  

"We talked for a long time and came to the same answer, We were in love 
with each other." 

"Well...I'm glad you two were finally able to tell each other how you 
felt." 

"You're not angry with us?" Said Sakura. 

"No! I'm happy for you two!"  

Sakura and Tomoyo's eyes lit up with joy.  

"Thank You mom!!" Said Tomoyo giving her mom a big hug.  

"Thank You." Said  Sakura, also giving her a hug.  

"So how did your dad take it Sakura?"  

"Well..." 

"We haven't told him yet." Interrupted Tomoyo. 

"I'm scared to tell him." Said Sakura. "I'm scared that he'll take it 
badly and forbid us from seeing each other." 

"I've known your dad for a long time Sakura. I think his reaction will 
be the same as mine. He just wants to see his daughter happy, no matter 
what." 

"Don't worry, It'll be fine. Tell you what...I'll go over to your place 
with you when you tell him. Ok?" 

"Thank You, that would be nice." 

"Now, lets drink this tea before it gets cold!" 

After having their tea, Tomoyo's mom drove them back to Sakura's house. 
When they got there they found out from Touya that her dad went to the 
market and would be back soon. So while they waited, Sakura fixed 
everyone a Sandwich. 

"Here you are." 

"Thanks Monster." 

"Just for that I'm going to eat yours too Touya." 

"Come on, I'm only teasing. Can I still have it please?"  

"Ok, here." 

"Thanks Monster." 

Sakura gave Touya a nasty look while Tomoyo and her mother laughed. 

"I'm Home!!" Said Sakura's dad as he walked in the door. 

"Hi dad! Here, let me help you with those." 

"Thanks Sakura." 

"Dad? We need to talk to you about something." 

"We?"  

"Hello." Said Tomoyo and her mom. 

"Hi." 

"Come join us." 

"Sure. What's this all about? Touya? Do you know?" 

"Nope, not yet." 

Sakura sat down on the couch next to Tomoyo and her mother. 

"Dad... Touya..." 

"Last night... when Tomoyo came over, she asked me if there was anything 
that I wanted to tell her. She knew that I had something on my mind and 
she was pretty sure what it was, with her and I being best friends for 
so long now. Her and I sat in my room and talked for what seemed to be 
hours. When we were finished, both her and I realized how the other 
felt." 

"Dad, Touya. Tomoyo and I..." 

"We're..."  

"It's ok Sakura dear." Reassured Tomoyo's mom. "Just tell them how you 
feel."  

Sakura sighed. "Tomoyo and I are in love." 

"That's great!" Said Sakura's dad. 

"So... who are the unfortunate souls that you two suckered in?" Remarked 
Touya. 

"Touya... are you really that dense?" Asked Sakura's dad. "They're not 
in love with two boys... they're in love with each other." 

"WHAT?!" Touya yells as he looks back at Sakura and Tomoyo, who's faces 
are deep red with embarrassment. 

"Well I can't speak for Touya, but I'm happy for you two." Says Sakura's 
dad.  

"Thanks dad... I wasn't sure how you would react to this, I was scared 
that you wouldn't like it. That's why Tomoyo's mom said that she would 
be with us when we told you." 

"Well, I guess this means we don't have to worry about you falling in 
love with that brat huh?"  

"He's not a brat!!" Sakura yelled to Touya. 

Touya was referring to when Sakura had a crush on Sayoran. 

The five of them talked for hours about love and relationships. Before 
they knew it, it was getting to be really late.  

"Oh my goodness!! Look at the time!!" Said Tomoyo. 

"Oh! Well we better get going." Said Tomoyo's mom. "Tomorrow's a school 
day, and you girls need your rest." 

"Ok." Said Tomoyo. "I'll see you tomorrow Sakura." 

"See you tomorrow." 

The two of them gave each other a goodnight kiss, and Tomoyo and her 
mother left for home.
