Tomoyo picked up the box that contained Sakura's outfit and gave it to

"You're going to look perfect in it Sakura-chan."

"Come in." 

"Sakura.... is Tomoyo in here?" 

"Yes. I'm here Touya. What is it?" 

"Can I talk to you downstairs for a minute?"

"Go ahead and change Sakura-chan. When you're done, come downstairs so

"Ok... may I ask what for?" 

"You'll see in a minute." Said Touya as he set up the Camera so he could

"I guess she wants me to be a Witch this year huh?" Sakura said

Sakura pulled out all of the peices to her costume. Or so she thought.

"Tomoyo-chan...." She said whining as she looked at the pair of black

"That's Tomoyo... she always has to co-ordinate everything." 

Sakura sighed and took off what remained of her clothes. She put on her

"What was that!?" Sakura said out loud. She walked over to the shower

"HOEEEEEEEEEEEEE!!!!" She screamed as she bolted from the bathroom. She

"What!? What is it Sakura-chan!?" 

"Sp...sp..." 

"Spider?" 

Tomoyo could feel Sakura shake her head yes and looked over at Touya,

"Gr...gre..ha,ha,ha,ha...." He couldn't even speak he was laughing so

Sakura was shaking with anger as she stood up. Touya and Kero took one

"Ha,ha,ha,ha,ha... look...look at her! This makes it even more perfect!" 

Sakura looked down and realized that she was only wearing the bra and

"Hello everyone! I'm back!" He said as he pulled in the last of his

"Gah! Sakura! What are you doing!? Why are you in your underwear!?" 

"Onii-chan.... I'm going to kill you!" She screamed as she lunged at 
him. Tomoyo thought fast and grabbed hold of Sakura's waist as she 
struggled to get free so she could give Touya the slow, agonizing death

of this little stunt. 

"Yeah right Kero-chan! You're probably the one that came up with this 
little prank, aren't you!?" 

"Calm down Sakura-chan!" Tomoyo said. 

She picked up a blanket off the back of the couch and wrapped it around 
Sakura as she led her back upstairs. She was going to lead her into her 
bedroom but Sakura broke free and stomped angrily into the bathroom. She 
threw back the shower curtain and tore the large, menacing spider to
