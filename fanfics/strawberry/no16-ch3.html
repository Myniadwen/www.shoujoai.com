"Something good that you wished for will come to you today."

Was what my horoscope said.

I never was the type to listen to it... but I did so today.

I hoped a little that it was true.

It started awfully...

Sei-senpai being annoying so early in the morning.

And soon... I've all but forgotten,

about that little message on that cheap morning newspaper.

I probably won't be reading my horoscope anytime soon...

It always seems to be wrong or the opposite for me.

You're probably asking why I say so...

Well...

The one person I'd probably expected never to see again...

Maybe...

Even wished never to see again...

Greeted me with today...

With that same heart breaking smile...

...from six years ago.

-Yaya
