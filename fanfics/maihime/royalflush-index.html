Royal Flush

a Mai HiME fanfiction by Kieli


Pairing: Natsuki/Shizuru
Last updated: 2008-12-09

This is sort of set in the time period between the end of the HiME Festival and just prior to graduation. Midori is up to her old tricks which cause a little bit of a challenge between Shizuru and Natsuki to emerge. The gauntlet gets thrown, clothes come off. Poor Natsuki. She never had a chance. It's a one shot but it will be in two parts only (hopefully fingers crossed) It's un-beta'd for reasons that are my own. If I missed any typos or extra words, you'll have to let me know. The story started out as a conversation between Lang and myself. The hilarious Kuga/Shizuru dialogue is all hers. I just filled in the blanks.
