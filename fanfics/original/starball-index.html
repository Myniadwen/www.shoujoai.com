Legends of Asgarth: The Starball Crystal

a Original Fiction fanfiction by Kaiser


Pairing: Daneira/Nephalie
Last updated: 2004-08-16

A medieval/fantasy type story. The country of Wingard has just recovered from a Thousand Year War between two large-scale families that have sought to lay claim to the throne. In this time of post-war peace, a young swordswoman & dragon hunter called 'Daneira' comes into contact with a slave girl named Nephalie. So begins an adventure that will led them to frightening revelations. Revelations that may change the world.
