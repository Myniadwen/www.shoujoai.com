Politically (in)Correct as Always

a Original Fiction fanfiction by David Rasmussen


Pairing: Eve/Keiko
Last updated: 2003-10-05

Once upon a time a writer named James Finn Garner took it upon himself to enlighten and liberate "classic" bedtime stories and retell them in a way that was much more in keeping with the society of today. This is the fallout that Keiko and must deal with as the dream continent of the GrimmLands is set into chaos in the aftermath of this "Event," that is if she doesn't let her personal life interfere with the "mission" at hand.
