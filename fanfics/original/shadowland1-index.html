A Shadowlander's Dream: Book 1 - A Tale of Shadows

a Original Fiction fanfiction by Kathryn K Williams


Pairing: Jenn/Kimberly, Kimberly/Natalie, Kimberly/Lauren, Jenn/Kris, Jenn/Tanya, Jenn/May, Jenn/Kima, Kima/Natalie, Alyson/Natalie, Jessica/Kris, Cherilyn/Tanya, Alyson/Nicole
Last updated: 2008-08-21

While trying to help a new classmate Jenn comes to realize that reality is not what it seems and suddenly has to face her past, a past that she had completely forgotten... until now. Enter a world of mystery and madness where nightmares are a reality and the only thing that can save you is accepting the darkness within your own heart and allowing yourself to love.
