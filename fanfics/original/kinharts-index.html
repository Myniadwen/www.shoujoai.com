Kindred Hearts

a Original Fiction fanfiction by Kaiton


Pairing: Aimee/Keiko
Last updated: 2005-11-10

Keiko's life has been ended prematurely, and her friends, Eric and Kylie are in mourning. Worst off, Aimee takes the loss of her only love hard, and has locked herself in her school housing room. Eric and Kylie are able to get her to move on a bit, but old memories bring a new dream. What does the future hold for this group?
