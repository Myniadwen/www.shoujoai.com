reason to stay in bed. The reality was she didn't want to go face the world.

      As the door opened, she turned over on her side, trying to pretend she was asleep. She

      "Are you still worried about your sisters?" Hoshi flinched. "I'm sure they don't blame 
you... you were under someone else's control, so-"

      "But that's not true," Hoshi whispered. "I allowed myself to be controlled because it 
gave me something to hide behind. As long as I could tell distance myself from my guilt,
