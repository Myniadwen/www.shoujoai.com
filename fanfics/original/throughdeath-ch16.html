      Abby had succumbed to her weariness, no longer having the stamina to stay up and wait 
for Sakura. She was surprised she could fall asleep with her lover missing. She drifted off, 
barely noticing anything around her. At first, everything was fine, but... it didn't take 
long until she heard the sound. Was it... flapping? Dare she say it, Abby thought there was 
a bird right outside her window.

      There was no chirping... just flapping. Abby buried herself deeper in the covers, 
wanting to sleep, wanting to wake up and see Sakura. The sounds stopped. Abby paid it no 
mind, prepared to go to sleep, but... she felt a hand slip between the covers and caress her 
waist. The young mother let out a happy sigh as it drifted over her clothed form.

      She started to awake as the bed sunk. There were two hands now and Abby had no doubt 
as to whom they belonged to. She cracked her eyes open, but they fluttered shut as an eager 
mouth kissed her exposed nape. "Sakura..." she murmured out, only to be greeted by silence. 
Even with her eyes closed, Abby could imagine the gentle smile on the other woman's face.

      Sakura climbed on top of her with grace and started to remove Abby's clothes. The 
blonde soon realized that it was a very naked Sakura on top of her. Just as she was about to 
open her eyes and greet her, Abby was swept up in a passionate kiss. Sakura was in a 
wonderful mood today; she could tell from the emotions pouring into the kiss.

      Their bodies melded together perfectly and Abby's eyes fluttered open to meet Sakura's 
own. At first, the woman's snowy white hair obscured her vision, but... Abby frowned. 
Something wasn't quite right. Sakura smiling at her, but that wasn't it. Her normally 
piercing red eyes... were no longer there. Instead, there were silver eyes staring back at 
her.

      Abby was positive she wasn't going crazy. "Sakura...?" The woman on top of her only 
seemed to smile wider at her confusion. "Yes?" Abby sighed as one hand caressed her face. 
She heard the flapping noise again, but this time, it was much closer. Abby pulled her lover 
down, only to find something on Sakura's shoulder blades.

      The blonde's unasked question was answered as two pure white wings came into view, 
feathers falling from them. "You... what happened?" Abby sat up and found herself being 
wrapped up in the pristine wings, feathers rustling against her body. "I've been negotiating 
my situation," Sakura murmured, caressing Abby's cheek. "I made a different kind of deal so 
you and I... can be together."

      Sakura laid her head on Abby's chest. "I... told you before that I didn't want us to 
be torn apart by time, so I..." The angel blushed. "I exchanged everything - my eyes, my 
armor, my immortality." Abby looked shocked. "Sakura, you didn't have to do that and your 
wings... how did this...?"

      "I've done such a competent job as the Living Inferno that my... superiors... allowed 
me to trade. Now, you and I will always be connected." Abby didn't quite understand. 
"That's... wonderful, but I still don't get it. How are we going to stay together?" Sakura 
grabbed Abby's hand and placed it on her chest. "Can you feel it? My heartbeat?"

      Abby nodded and Sakura made the blonde woman feel her own. She was startled when she 
realized that her and Sakura's hearts beat at the same time. "This way... if one of us 
dies... then we'll go together." Abby let it sink in before smiling. "I... wouldn't have it 
any other way..."

      Sakura gently pushed her back down. Abby was laughing quietly. "That still doesn't 
explain your wings..." The angel blushed. "You... you said you wanted to feel them, so I got 
them back for you." Abby ran a hand through one of her wings and Sakura shuddered in 
pleasure.

      "They're rather sensitive..." The white haired woman whispered, her face flushed. 
"Tell me," Abby whispered against Sakura's lips. "Is this all a dream?"
