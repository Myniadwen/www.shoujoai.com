The Messiah Series

a Original Fiction fanfiction by Kaiser


Pairing: Chizuru/Yumi, Tomoko/Yumi, Ashura/Leena, Aratia/Lune
Last updated: 2006-03-06

In a world 'despoiled by human corruption' and blighted by war, a sole political body rules over the innocent -- Protocol. Only one tragic woman can stand against them -- Yumi Mishima. Yet Protocol is only the beginning of her road... one that will change not only her life but those of every other living human being... until the day that 'man' must answer to 'God'... (Co-written with Shadow Bunny and Christine).
