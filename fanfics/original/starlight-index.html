Starlight

a Original Fiction fanfiction by Nutzoide


Pairing: Tataku/Yae, Angelica/Jei
Last updated: 2003-11-21

After an attack on her ship a cargo hauler tries to finish off the job she started, but the captain of her now dead attackers tags along, meaning a guard needs to be hired, as does a new ship and its pilots. And then there's the student her employer wants to send along for the job.
