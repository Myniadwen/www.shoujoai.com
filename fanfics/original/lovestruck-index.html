LoveStruck

a Original Fiction fanfiction by Jennifer Poulos


Pairing: Heart/Seraph
Last updated: 2004-12-21

Harteriel Serafina, known as Heart to her friends, is an angel; a Virtue, First Class, more precisely. Or at least she used to be. She's been exiled to the Earth for committing an unforegiveable sin: she has fallen in love with someone she really shouldn't have... An agent of Hell. But that should have been the end ot it, so why are both Heaven and Hell so concerned about the situation that they are plotting major interventions? [LIME]
