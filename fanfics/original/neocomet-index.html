Neo Comet Swordsman Setsuya: Second Origin

a Original Fiction fanfiction by Kaiser


Pairing: Ayame/Julia
Last updated: 2005-03-14

Part 1 in the Neo Comet Swordsman Series. In the year of 2081, the world has been irreparably damaged by the landing of something that comes to be known as the 'Genesis Comet'. Technology as you know it is a thing of the past. And a last ditch attempt at salvaging life is produced as the leaders of the world agree to create things called 'Safe Zones'. Countries protected from the ruined outside world by magical barriers. But such actions cannot hide the darkness that comes from a world rife with suffering. A powerful conglomerate arises with a kind of trans-national power that threatens to do more damage than even the Genesis Comet. They are known as the Shougatsu Financial Group. And as their intentions reach a zenith, the leader of the corporation announces a tournament called Ten Sekai Sentou Kyousou. But what is the secret behind the tournament? What are the true intentions of the Shougatsu Financial Group's leader? And can anyone stop them before they bring catastrophe to an already battered world? Or is this just the first stage in an even greater saga?
