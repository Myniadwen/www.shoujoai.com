Super Heroine Showdown 2004: Forbidden Tokyo

a Original Fiction fanfiction by Kaiser


Pairing: Rinako/Umi, Esperanza/Keiya, Kumi/Shinryu
Last updated: 2005-03-14

A sort of crossover from many of my original fictions (Rival Rage, Neo Comet Swordsman Saizaki, Magic Star Warriors, Prophecy of Krishinda, Magical Sword Princess Aoi, etc). A five strong team of warriors called the Silver Valkyries have been gathered together from multiple realities to bring down an evil group of demons called the Jikininki. And to protect a young princess known as 'Umi' who will gain untold powers when she turns eighteen. Of course, in the long run, there might be deeper issues that will prevent them from carrying that out. Will Rinako and her new buddies ever get to go home...?
