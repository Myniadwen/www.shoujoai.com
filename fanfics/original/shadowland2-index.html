A Shadowlander's Dream: Book 2 - A Tale of Sorrows

a Original Fiction fanfiction by Kathryn K Williams


Pairing: Jenn/Kimberly, Kimberly/Natalie, Kimberly/Lauren, Jenn/Kris, Jenn/Tanya, Jenn/May, Jenn/Kima, Kima/Natalie, Alyson/Natalie, Jessica/Kris, Cherilyn/Tanya, Alyson/Nicole
Last updated: 2008-08-21

It is called Whispering Hills Academy, a strange all-girls school surrounded by a dreaded dark forest at the edge of reality. Few would venture near the grounds of this foreboding school, however it has become home too many lost souls. Girls with pasts that they would rather forget as they find themselves lost with no one but each other to confide in.
