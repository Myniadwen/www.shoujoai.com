Black Rose, Blue Thunder

a Ranma 1/2 fanfiction by Dreiser


Pairing: Akane/Mousse, Kasumi/Perfume
Last updated: 2004-11-16

Tired of canon? Want to read about a reality where anything can and does happen? Then this fanfic is for you. But wait, you want an example of the insanity? Lets run down the list... Ranma is a charming con artist with a killer smile; Akane is the nicest and most kawaii girl in all of Nerima; while Nabiki is considered the violent one in the Tendo family. But lets not forget about Genma. Lucky him, he changes into a big fat flying pig. And that's just a small taste of the utter weirdness of this fic. Sounds fun, doesn't it? So get reading already!
