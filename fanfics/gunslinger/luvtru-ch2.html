Rico walked into the room next to where Henrietta and Triela were. The 
room was slightly smaller then their room but she didn't care.

"Henrietta is so lucky," Rico said to herself.

She tossed herself onto the bed and laid there. She stared at the 
ceiling. The room started spinning and Triela's voice was the only thing 
she could hear.

                          ~~~~~~~~~~

Triela and Henrietta heard a sound come from the hall and they got up to 
go check it out.

"Hello???" Triela said poking her head out of the door.

Henrietta walked over and knocked on Rico's door, "Rico was it you that we 
heard out here?"

                           ~~~~~~~~~~

Rico just laid on the bed. She couldn't hear a thing going on around 
her. She sat up on the bed and swayed back and forth.

Something was wrong with her but she couldn't tell. Something in the 
house had done something to her, but she had no idea.

                           ~~~~~~~~~~

Henrietta pounded on the door. Triela walked up behind her and grabbed 
her hand, "I guess it was nothing love, just our imagination." She wrapped 
her arms around Henrietta.

Hennrietta smiled, "Okay, I guess you're right."

They walked back into their bedroom Triela's arms still around 
Henrietta.

They heard a thud come from the room next to them and they bolted out of 
their room once again.

                            ~~~~~~~~~~

Rico had fallen to the floor and lay unconscious.

                             ~~~~~~~~~~

Henrietta and Triela pounded on Rico's door, but nothing happened.

"I'm going in," Henrietta took a gun out of her belt and shot a bullet 
through the handle of the door. She ripped into the room and ran over to 
Rico. "What the hell kind of show is this???"

Triela ran in behind Henrietta and looked over her shoulder.

                          ~~~~~~~~~~

Toya then rushed in and over to Rico, "She came into the room as planned 
ladies and gents. She is unconscious and her friends came for her!!! Truly 
a beautiful job."

Henrietta and Triela look at each other in confusion. Triela grabbed Toya 
and looked into his eyes, "What did you do to her???"

Henrietta stared down at him meekly watching Triela, "We just sprayed a 
kind of gas in here to make her fall unconcious!!! and It Worked!" Toya 
released himself from her grip.

                         ~~~~~~~~~~

"We'll find them," Guiseppe spoke into his cellphone.

"You better those girls are all we have in our section," a voice came 
through.

 "I'll get them."

                         ~~~~~~~~~~

Triela and Henrietta stood there while people they didn't know put their 
hands all over Rico.
