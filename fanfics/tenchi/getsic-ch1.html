much as an inch. Both of them cried and held each other closely. Then,
just as the sun went down, Mihoshi looked up into Kiyone's eyes and
reached her hand up to caress her partner's cheek. "I love you
Kiyone... I have for so long." Kiyone put her hand over Mihoshi's
holding it to her cheek. "I know Mihoshi... I love you too." Mihoshi's
hands slid behind Kiyone's neck while Kiyone's wrapped around her
waist. Slowly they both leaned in and kissed.
