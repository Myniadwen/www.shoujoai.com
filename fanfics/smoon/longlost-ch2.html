Normal P.O.V

Crystal’s first thoughts were ‘ok what just happened here why don’t I 
remember anything’ getting up on shaky legs she saw the girls walking 
back. But what she saw between Amara and Serena did not go unnoticed. 
From what she could see of the girls it looked like they all caught 
it. And deep inside there was apart of Crystal that was happy that 
they where starting to remember their past together. But that was 
apart that Crystal did not know about yet. When they looked up they 
saw Crystal and the worry that was in her eyes, though she did not 
remember what happened after she past out she did remember that they 
had started to run in the direction that the attack was in. meeting 
half way three things came out of her mouth “are you guys ok? What in 
the world did you think you where doing scaring me like that? And 
don’t you EVER DO THAT AGAIN.” Ending in a scream, the Sailors looked 
at her closely and noticed that like Sailor Ice her eyes where a dark 
brown almost black, and her eyes only seemed to get darker as she got 
angrier, as they got closer to her she did start to calm down as she 
saw that they were not hurt. To them she was your normal girl with 
short light brown hair with blond highlights. Serena and Amara being 
the closest to her where able to calm her down even more. “Crystal 
please we are ok, please calm down.”
