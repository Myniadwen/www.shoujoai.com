Mars froze as all the circuits in her brain tripped. Her body stiffened 
as she became more than aware of the slim form she held in her arms. Her 
blood rushed in her ears, and even her breathing sounded like a dull 
roar.

'What am I doing? Dear God, what am I doing?'

Her eyes closed, and she kissed her princess back. Everything seemed to 
stop, and Mars felt as though she were holding Serenity for an eternity.

A sound somewhere at the end of the corridor startled them both, and the 
blonde jumped out of her arms, looking just as shocked as Rei.

"Rei...I'm so sorry..." she whispered, tears falling from her beautiful 
blue eyes. "I don't know what came over me."

Mars was locked in dumbfounded shock. All she could do was stare at her 
princess, mouth agape.

"I'm sorry!" Serenity choked out, tearing her eyes away from Rei's. She 
disappeared into her room, slamming the door behind her.

"...huh?" Mars asked the door, her mind reeling. Two and two began to

"Wait...what?" she cried, almost angry now.

She moved forward, fully intent on entering the princess' room, but

Jupiter and Mercury rounded the corner, laughing quietly. Jupiter halted

"Ah, sorry Rei. Didn't know you were standing there." Jupiter mumbled

"Are you alright Rei? You look like you've seen a ghost."

Inside, Mars cracked. She wanted to laugh, scream and cry, all while

Her fellow soldiers flushed again.

'That should stop the questions.'

But Jupiter had one more. "Where's Serenity?"

"Sleeping!" Mars blurted out, a little too quickly for her liking. She

Mercury nodded. "You look like you really need it."

'NO KIDDING!'

'Venus? Do I know a Venus?'

"No! Ah, no. I'm ok. Just really tired. I'll go catch up with Minako. 
Goodnight."

"Goodnight." they responded in unison as Mars took off down the hall.

"Huh." Jupiter muttered after she had gone.

'I feel homesick. Homesick for something I'm not even familiar with. If

"Rei? It's me, can I come in?"

'Bloody brilliant!! What are you doing for your next trick, Wonder

"That's all? You're just stressed? Funny. I've seen you stressed, and I

'I'm going to close my eyes, and count to ten. When I open them, I'll be

'Come on, Powers That Be. Have I been that evil?'

Venus took a step back. "W...why?"

"Because." the Martian choked out. "I ruin everything I touch, and I've

Minako said nothing, but the look on her face already spoke volumes of

Rei felt a jarring pain in her chest. Her heart crawled up into her

The blonde stood, locked in a stunned silence.

'Speechless. I made her speechless. An otherwise impossible feat.'

Mars met her eyes at last, feeling almost positive that this would be

"Minako, I walked Serenity to her room today, after we all came in.

Venus was still rooted to her spot, head bent down, and Rei felt her

"Minako, say something, please..."

"What can I say?" the blonde whispered faintly.

Mars could hear the tears in her voice, was sure she just felt her heart

"Don't." Minako whispered dangerously. "I've heard that line before.

"I didn't do it to hurt you!!"

Venus looked her straight in the eyes. "You know what hurts the most

'You idiot.'

Minako was still glaring at her angrily, while Rei was flooded with such
