Long Lost Love Returned

a Sailor Moon fanfiction by Crystal Rose Winter


Pairing: Haruka/Usagi
Last updated: 2008-07-23

After beating all of their enemies the Scouts just want to live a normal life but the presence of a new set of memories naming Haruka and Usagi lovers and Mamoru the bad guy that brought down the Moon Kingdom, at hand. What will the Sailors do when all of this happens and they see a strange Scout that is calling herself Sailor Ice Leader of the Outer Sailor Scouts all at once? What does all this mean? Find out in Long Lost Love Returned. Fic uses dub names.
