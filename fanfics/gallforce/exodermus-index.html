Gall Force: Project Exodermus

a Gall Force fanfiction by Quanto


Pairing: Catty/Rica
Last updated: 2008-07-08

1st Part in a complete retelling of Gall Force. Starts with the events leading up to the creation of Mission 21. Influenced strongly by the events outlined in the short Manga "Blue Sister" by Kenichi Sonoda. A young Catty Nebulart must lay down the plans for the future Mission 21 while balancing out time for her close friend Rica.
