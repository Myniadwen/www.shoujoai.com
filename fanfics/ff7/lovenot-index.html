Love Not Often

a Final Fantasy 7 fanfiction by Bhryn Astaire


Pairing: Aeris/Tifa
Last updated: 2006-10-26

Tifa is a girl who's past has been steeped in violence and loss. Aerith is a girl who has suffered persecution because of her heritage. Both girls are drawn together by the Destiny to save and change the world, but along the way they find in each other the will to become better people, to be loved exactly as they are and the best friend both girls never knew they were missing.
