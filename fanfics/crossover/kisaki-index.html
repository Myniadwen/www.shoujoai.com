Mai Kisaki

a Crossovers fanfiction by Ange Sinistre


Pairing: Natsuki/Shizuru, Aoi/Chie, Haruka/Yukino
Last updated: 2008-01-13

Crossover with Mai HiME and Mai Otome. Sequel to "The Kyoto Hunt". A ruined Earth calls Garderobe home again. There, an ancient war between HiME and Otome still rages. When our heroes arrive, they must make their choice: Stay loyal to the Otome, or embrace their HiME origins? Something darker still rests in Kyoto...
