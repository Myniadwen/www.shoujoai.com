a Non-Anime Fanfiction fanfiction by David Rasmussen


Pairing: Alicia/Hermione, Angelica/Katie
Last updated: 2006-12-30

[Harry Potter] A "Remastered" style Harry Potter storyline NOT focusing on Potter, based on Year 3 ("Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban"). Rogue Raven "Alicia Spinner" continues her masquerade as the girl she accidentally killed three years earlier (Alicia Spinnet) and continues her "learning" at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft & Wizardry while struggling to develop a relationship with one of the girls there, unaware that the recent prison break from Azkaban released something else that may be her undoing.
