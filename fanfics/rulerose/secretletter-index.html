a Rule of the Rose fanfiction by Kathryn K Williams


Pairing: Jennifer/Wendy
Last updated: 2008-08-21

Follow Jennifer and experience the tragedy that is her life. During a trip with her parents on an Air ship disaster happens and in which she is the soul survivor. Unwanted she is sent to an Orphanage in the middle of the woods called "The Rose Garden", however along the way she encounters a strange man and her life goes from bad to worse.
