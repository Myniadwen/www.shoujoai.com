Red and Black

a Noir fanfiction by Kirika


Pairing: Kirika/Mireille
Last updated: 2008-05-06

Their pilgrimage for the past is over; only the present now matters... but for those who walk the dark path of murder all peace is fleeting. A new enemy arises to threaten Mireille and Kirika's quiet existence, one who wishes an old legacy to be reborn. The swords of Noir are forced to be drawn against the taint of the world, wielded by the true Black Hands, and Mireille and Kirika must now skirt that fine line... between red and black.
