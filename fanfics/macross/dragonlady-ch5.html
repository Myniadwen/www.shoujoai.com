talking."

"No," Lisa said, "I'm not sure about this, but I'm listening to my heart 
and my heart says it wants to dance with you."

"Okay, but one question," Ranma said, smiling demurely.

"What?" Lisa asked.

"Who leads?" Ranma giggled. Lisa pondered this for a moment and reached 
an answer.

"I lead now," Lisa said with seriousness. "You can lead the other way." 
Ranma gave her a heart-stopping smile and nodded as she was swept into 
the taller woman's arms. They began to sway about the floor with 
matching smiles.
